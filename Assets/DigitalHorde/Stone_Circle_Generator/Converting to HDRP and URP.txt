Thank you for purchasing my Stone Circle Generator!

The asset comes with built-in materials but these can easily be converted to either URP or HDRP materials using the standard method below:

1. Select all materials in the Assets/VarcanRex/Stone_Circle_Generator/Materials folder.
2. Click the Edit -> Rendering -> Materials -> Convert Selected Built-in Materials to HDRP/URP

Then just wait for the conversion to complete, since their are 70+ materials it may take some time.
